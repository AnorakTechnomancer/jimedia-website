About Me

Hey, I'm Jim.

I'm a filmmaker and photographer based in Illinois with a thing for live music and the stories that happen when the lights go down. I've been shooting bands and covering shows for years, and I still get the same rush every time a set kicks off.

"I don't just point a camera at something — I try to find the frame that makes you feel like you were there."

Beyond concert photography, I work across the full spectrum of media production — scripting, directing, editing, color grading, and everything in between. Big venue or basement show, I show up ready to make something worth keeping.

I work independently, which means you get one person who genuinely cares about your project from the first conversation to the final delivery. No middlemen, no cookie-cutter packages — just me, my gear, and a commitment to getting it right.
